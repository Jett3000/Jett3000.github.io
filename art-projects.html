June 2023

Nerve Sampling

Generates faux nervous systems by generating a tree with poisson disc sampling. Patterns are influenced by seven parameters affecting and modulating the search annulus.

Make your own with this generator.

May 2023

Bubbles

Something like a generative washing machine. Shapes are laid out in a Processing sketch and exported as SVG for plotting.

Plasma Topology

A combination of 2D perlin noise, linear algebra and randomness mimicking topographic lines. Runs in realtime as a GLSL shader.

April 2023

Pink Topology

An earlier experiment with perlin noise topological fields, inspired by Pop Box Gallery's We Don't Care exhibit. An animated version was displayed at the exhibit's opening reception as a projection installation.

Minecraft Party

Whipped up a few hours before one of the best birthday parties I've attended, this p5.js sketch welcomed party goers with a kinetic homage to a classic game.

Warped Noise
